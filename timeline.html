Key Points in Michael Jordan's Life

  • February 17, 1963 Michael Jordan was born in NYC, in the US.
  • April 9, 1977 Michael Jordan goes to high school!
  • May 20, 1981 Michael Jordan goes to college.
  • April 27, 1984 The start of Michael Jordan's early years as a professional basketball player.
  • October 6, 1993 Michael Jordan retires for the first time and starts a short baseball career in the Chicago White Sox AA minor league team.
  • March 12, 1995 Michael Jordan returns to the NBA to continue his basketball career.
  • January 13, 1999 Michael Jordan announces his second retirement.
  • September 25, 2001 Michael Jordan returns to the NBA once again to join the Washington Wizards.
  • April 26, 2003 Michael Jordan plays his last basketball game.
